WHY TUNEWAVE?

TuneWave was made initially as a project for my university, which later turned into a challenge to make a website using only HTML, CSS and Javascript. The idea is to improve your musical & lyrical knowledge all while having fun and competing against friends & people online. More ideas & more features are to come in the near future so stay tuned!
